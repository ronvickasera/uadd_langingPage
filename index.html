About Urban Data Analytics

Rate of urbanisation in Madhya Pradesh is fast reaching the National average of 28%. It is expected to cross the 30% mark in another 15 years. Most of the increase in urban population in the state is driven by an increase in the number of habitations entering the urban strata rather than an increase in the population of existing urban areas.
